What we offer

Our Global Employee Assistance Programme provides proactive mental health and wellbeing support to employees in one hundred and eighty plus countries and fifty plus languages. Staff receive structured counselling, digital therapy, critical incident help and personal wellbeing tools on a single secure platform.

Key benefits

24 / 7 multilingual helpline

Employees can speak with qualified counsellors anytime by phone or video in more than fifty languages.

Up to 12 structured counselling sessions

Face-to-face, virtual or phone sessions delivered by master’s-level clinicians for every presenting issue.

Digital CBT & self-help modules

Evidence-based programmes covering anxiety, burnout and resilience, accessible on mobile and web.

Wellbeing analytics & ROI reporting

Live dashboards reveal usage trends, risk areas and value to help HR leaders make data-driven decisions.

Legal & financial guidance

Practical advice on debt, wills, tenancy, consumer rights and more, reducing employee stress fast.

Family, childcare & elder-care support

Specialists source nurseries, carers and educational resources so staff stay focused at work.

Crisis & trauma response

On-site and virtual support for critical incidents such as accidents, natural disasters or workplace violence.

Dedicated account management

A named team guides rollout, promotion and ongoing optimisation to maximise engagement.

Why it matters

Organisations that invest in wellbeing outperform those that do not. Our programme lifts satisfaction, lowers absenteeism and raises productivity while meeting duty of care obligations. More than thirty five million users already rely on this solution.

Who it is for

The service suits multinational employers, public bodies and fast growing firms that prioritise mental health and resilience. Whether you have ten or ten thousand staff, we deliver global cover with local impact.

Frequently asked questions

How is our EAP different from other models?

We know what it takes to cultivate a healthy work culture. Available via one convenient app, in person and by phone, making it easy for employers to provide timely access to an established, large and diverse network of counsellors whenever employees need help.

What qualifications do your care counsellors have?

Every one of our counselling professionals has either a master’s or doctorate in psychology, clinical social work, marriage and family therapy, or a related mental-health field. They all hold at least three years’ post-master clinical experience—preferably within EAP—and a minimum of 2 500 hours of professional counselling practice.

How does this EAP keep employee health information safe?

Participant data is stored in our secure case-management system, hosted in on-premise data centres in the United States and Canada, and in Microsoft Azure for Europe/UK and Australian clients. Compliance certifications include SOC 1, SOC 2, SOC 3, ISO 27001 and HITRUST.

Backed by proven impact

Independent feedback shows lower turnover, higher engagement and stronger mental health across every sector we serve. Clients report a fifty per cent fall in poor mental health ratings and a thirty eight per cent drop in resenteeism hours within months.
